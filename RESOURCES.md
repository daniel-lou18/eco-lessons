# Business Engine & Fundamental Analysis — Resources

Curated, high-trust sources for this course. Knowledge in lessons should be
drawn from here, not from memory. Communities are where wisdom is tested.

## Knowledge

- [SEC — Beginners' Guide to Financial Statements](https://www.sec.gov/about/reports-publications/investorpubsbegfinstmtguide)
  The regulator's own plain-English overview of the four statements and how they
  interrelate. Use for: the canonical definition of each statement; grounding
  claims about what revenue, net income, and cash flow officially mean.

- [Aswath Damodaran — "Cash Flows vs. Earnings" (Foundations, Session 3 slides, PDF)](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/pdfiles/FoundationsOnline/slides/session3.pdf)
  NYU Stern's "Dean of Valuation" on exactly why accounting earnings ≠ cash
  flows (accruals + capital-vs-operating expense classification). Use for: the
  Profit ≠ Cash Flow lesson; the three-step bridge from earnings to cash flow.

- [Aswath Damodaran — The Little Book of Valuation: Cash Flows](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/New_Home_Page/littlebook/cashflows.htm)
  Accessible chapter site defining free cash flow to equity / to the firm. Use
  for: free cash flow definitions and why FCF approximates "potential dividends."

- [Aswath Damodaran — Valuation, Chapter 9: Measuring Earnings (PDF)](https://pages.stern.nyu.edu/~adamodar/pdfiles/valn2ed/ch9.pdf)
  Deeper treatment of how reported earnings must be adjusted (leases, R&D,
  one-time charges) before they mean anything. Use for: earnings-quality and
  "the numbers can be misleading" topics later in the course.

- [Corporate Finance Institute — Reading Financial Statements (free course)](https://corporatefinanceinstitute.com/course/learn-to-read-financial-statements-free-course/)
  Structured video course built around real annual reports (e.g. Starbucks cash
  flow statement). Use for: a guided walk-through when the learner is ready to
  read a real filing end-to-end.

- [Investopedia — Fundamental Analysis hub](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/f/fundamentalanalysis.asp)
  Reliable quick-reference for individual term definitions and worked examples.
  Use for: sanity-checking a single definition; never as the sole source for a
  claim.

- [SEC EDGAR — full-text filing search](https://www.sec.gov/cgi-bin/srqsb?text=10-K) · [EDGAR home](https://www.sec.gov/edgar/search/)
  Free primary source for real 10-K / 10-Q filings. Use for: practice — pulling
  the actual statements of a company the learner already knows as a customer.
